If you (very unlikely) have discovered a new BUG or just have a great idea - pronounced patches ;-) then please mail libwww@w3.org noting the version of the Library.

See also the Working List to see what we are planning to do. You can also find a list of bugs under the CERN Server, the Arena Browser, and the Line Mode Browser.
